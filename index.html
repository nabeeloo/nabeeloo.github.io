Business simulations

Having strategic client conversations based on industry disruptors

Nabeel pioneered an experiential, social-collaborative and competitive business simulation focused on industry disruptors. This differs from normal business simulations in two ways:

1) The focus is on having strategic client conversations, as opposed to running a business; and

2) Teams are different company profiles that realistically represent the current marketplace, as opposed to all companies having the same profile

Result The program beat 50+ similar learning offerings for top ratings by learner
Result Creation of 5 (and counting) unique business simulations by industry
Result Nearly 1,000 participants in 2018 alone

Neuroscience of trusted relationships

Practical insights from neuroscience

Nabeel conceived of a new way to use neuroscience to inform building trusted business relationships. Nabeel and team developed a video series with Wharton neuroscience professor Dr Michael Platt.

The videos are based on recent brain imaging studies that suggest our brain is wired to be social. To build good relationships, we need to understand how to tap into that "social brain" network to maximize connectivity between individuals.

Result Most sought after learning content related to business development
Result Invited talks and published article on the collaboration and video series
Result A deeper relationship with Dr Platt to create a "Neuroscience of ..." series

Learning analytics

Collect data. Classify data.

Nabeel uses a 3-tier model to improve learning through analytics:

1) Use of predictive factors and artificial intelligence (AI) for aggregate analysis of learning data to inform what topics are ripe for a learning intervention

2) Treating the learning experience like a product, focused on correlating user flow for optimal learning design.

3) Getting in the "black box" of the learning asset itself, utilizing metrics akin to website analytics to improve the quality of the learning experience.

Result 50% more accurate justification of topics to focus on for learning
Result 80% faster ability to customize learning campaign goals via web analytics tools
Result 30% improved learning design and earlier identification of potential design remediations
